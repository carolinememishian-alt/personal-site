About

Background

I'm an MBA candidate at MIT Sloan, where I'm a Dean's Fellow and pursuing a certificate in Product Management. I studied Computer Science at Vanderbilt with a minor in Engineering Management, which first got me interested in how technical and business decisions come together.


Before business school, I spent four years at Accenture as a management consultant, then joined Glassdoor. There, I worked across go-to-market strategy, strategic and business operations, and later transitioned into product management on the Employer Branding team.


Those experiences shaped how I think about building products—grounded in data, centered on users, and focused on driving real impact. I'm especially drawn to roles where I can stay close to both the problem and the people using the product, and help turn ambiguous ideas into something tangible.
