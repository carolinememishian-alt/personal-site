About

Background

I'm a first-year MBA student at MIT Sloan with roots in engineering and a career that's taken me from management consulting to tech. I studied Computer Science and Engineering Management at Vanderbilt University's School of Engineering, which gave me a foundation that sits at the intersection of technical and business thinking.


Before business school, I spent time at Accenture as a Management Consultant before joining Glassdoor's Business Operations team, where I worked across GTM strategy, Strategic Operations, and Product Strategy — and finished with a PM rotation on the Product team. Those experiences shaped how I think about building products: with rigor, user empathy, and a bias toward impact.
